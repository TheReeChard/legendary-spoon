Creating with PlayWard is as easy as 1, 2, 3.

Ready to create a great prompt? Follow the steps below and choose Quick Create to save time with fewer settings, or select Customized Prompt for a personalized result that requires a bit more effort.

1

Answer the on-screen questions to tailor the prompt's specific goals and features.

2

Double-check your settings. Use the icons on the left to edit any details, or hit "Generate" if you're ready.

3

Click "Copy and Open" to grab the text and automatically open the best AI for the job. Paste the prompt into the AI chat and send. Since AI can be unpredictable, review the results and feel free to re-run the prompt if it needs a second pass.
