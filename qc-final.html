Review/Edit the Quick Create Prompt

When your prompt is generated, select the "Copy and Open" button. The prompt will be copied and the recommended AI prompt engine will be opened in a new browser window. From there, simply select the AI's chat function and paste the prompt. Once you hit the send button, the AI will generate your activity! Make sure to check the final product for accuracy.
