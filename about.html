About Us

PlayWard is an AI-powered portal created by Ball State's EMDD program in partnership with Child's Play and Lurie Children's Hospital. Originally prototyped to help caregivers quickly generate personalized play materials for pediatric patients, the tool evolved through extensive testing and expert collaboration into the platform it is today.
